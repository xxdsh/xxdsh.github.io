1442. Count Triplets That Can Form Two Arrays of Equal XOR(形成两个异或相等的三元组)

方法一:Brute Force

Enumerate all tuples of (i, j, k), for each tuple (i, j, k), it takes O(n) time to compute xor
Time complexity: O(n^4)
Space complexity: O(1)

方法二：Brute Fore w / prefix

利用异或的性质
xors[i] = A[0] ^ …… ^ A[i - 1]
A[i] ^ …… ^ A[j] = (A[0] ^ …… ^ A[i - 1] ) ^ (A[0] ^ …… ^ A[i - 1]) ^ (A[i] ^ …… ^ A[j] ) = xors[i] ^ xors[j + 1]
A[i] ^ …… ^ A[j - 1] = xors[i] ^ xors[j]
A[j] ^ …… ^ A[k] = xors[j] ^ xors[k + 1]
Time complexity: O(n^3)
Space complexity: O(n)

方法三：Tuple to pair

a = b => a ^ b = 0
a ^ 0 = a

A[i] ^ …… ^ A[j - 1] = A[j] ^ …… ^ A[k]
A[i] ^ …… ^ A[j - 1] ^ A[j] ^ …… ^ A[k] = 0
A[0] ^ …… ^ A[i - 1] = A[0] ^ …… ^ A[k]
只需要找到i和k
Problem => find all pairs of (i – 1, k) such that xors[k+1] == xors[i]
For each pair (i – 1, k), there are k – i positions we can insert j.

Time complexity: O(n^2)
Space complexity: O(1)

方法四：HashTable

Similar to target sum, use a hashtable to store the frequency of each prefix xors.
Time complexity: O(n)
Space complexity: O(n)

190. Reverse Bits(颠倒二进制位)

Base-10 time case:
ans = ans * 10 + n % 10
n = n / 10
Base-2 is exact the same iff n is positive:
ans = ans * 2 + n % 2
n = n / 2
or use bit operators:
ans = (ans << 1) | (n & 1)
n = n >> 1
But need to handle leading zeros
00110 -> 01100 not 011
Time complexity: O(logn)
